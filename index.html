About Me

With over 6 years of experience in the entertainment industry, Sravan Kumar Randhi has made a mark as a versatile actor and model. His journey began in 2018 in India, where he honed his craft across print campaigns, fashion shoots, and indie films. In 2021, he took his talents to the global stage by relocating to Paris, France, expanding his presence in both entertainment and fashion industries.

Known for his strong screen presence and emotional depth, Sravan brings authenticity and dedication to every project. His ability to transform and adapt has made him a sought-after talent in the industry, capable of bringing any character to life with compelling performances.
